About Metalyol

Engineered steel. Built to last.

Metalyol is a vertically integrated structural-steel partner based in Istanbul. Since 2008 we have engineered, fabricated and erected the steel skeletons behind landmark projects across MENA, Europe and Africa — under one accountable team.

Our story

Three disciplines, one signature.

Metalyol started in 2008 as a steel trading desk on the European side of Istanbul. As clients began asking us to fabricate the sections we supplied, we built a 18,000 m² shop in İkitelli OSB. As the same clients asked us to erect what we fabricated, we trained, equipped and certified our own erection crews.

Today the three disciplines — trading, fabrication, erection — sit under one contract and one accountable team. There are no subcontracted gaps where a quality slip can hide. Every part of the path from mill to surveyed structure is owned in-house.

It is an unusual structure for a steel company. It is also why our clients keep coming back.

What we stand for

Three commitments. Held to the same tolerance as the steel we ship.

01

Engineering over opinion

Tolerance, weld procedure, fatigue envelope — we design every connection on the calc, not the personality in the room. Decisions are documented and repeatable.

02

One contract, three disciplines

Trading, fabrication and erection are owned in-house. There is no procurement gap, no fabrication outsourcing, no surprise erector on the site. One signature owns the path.

03

Safety as the baseline

ISO 45001 processes, full-time HSE manager on every active site, zero-LTI culture. Incident metrics ship with each project handover dossier — numbers, not slogans.

Certifications & standards

Documented compliance, not a wall of badges.

The steel we ship is welded to AWS D1.1 and EN 1090 EXC4, designed to AISC and Eurocode, and produced under ISO management systems for quality, safety and welding QA.

  • ISO 9001 Quality management system
  • ISO 45001 Occupational health & safety
  • ISO 3834-2 Welding quality requirements
  • EN 1090 EXC4 Steel structure execution class
  • AWS D1.1 Structural welding code — steel
  • AISC American Institute of Steel Construction
