Why Metalyol

The discipline of a manufacturer.
The agility of a partner.

01

Vertically integrated

Mill, fabrication and erection — owned in-house. No subcontracted gaps where quality slips.

Our trading desk negotiates direct mill allocation in Türkiye and Europe. Our 18,000 m² Istanbul fabrication yard handles cutting, welding, blasting and coating in-house. Our erection crews lift on site under our own HSE policy.

One contract. One signature. One accountable team across all three disciplines.

02

Engineered for tolerance

BIM-driven shop drawings, 3D-scanned QC, AWS D1.1 / EN 1090 EXC4 — verified, not promised.

Every assembly is modelled in Tekla, scanned post-fabrication on a metrology arm, and dimensioned against the source BIM. Full-penetration welds are 100% ultrasonically tested. Reports ship with each delivery.

We document the tolerance because we hit it — it's not a marketing line.

03

Built on landmark trust

Repeat partnership with engineers behind Lusail, Katara and Marina Yacht Club.

Recurring clients include Qatari Diar, Lusail Real Estate, Katara Hospitality, Raffles · Fairmont and Public Works Authority. Repeat business is our most reliable measure of quality.

If the engineers who specified the original landmarks are coming back for the next one, the structure speaks for itself.

04

Safety without exception

ISO 45001 processes, zero-tolerance LTI culture, full-time HSE on every active site.

A full-time HSE manager is present on every active site. Daily toolbox meetings, weekly audits, monthly reviews. Zero LTIs tolerated.

Numbers, not slogans — we publish our incident metrics on every project handover dossier.
